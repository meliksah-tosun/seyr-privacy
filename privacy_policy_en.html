Seyr – Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 13, 2025

Seyr is a mindfulness app designed to support your inner journey. This page explains how your personal data is processed when you use Seyr.

Controller

Makeryapı – Contact: meliksah@makeryapi.com

Data We Collect

  • Account / Sign‑in: Passworded access or Sign in with Apple. When using Sign in with Apple, your name and email may be provided (with your consent).
  • In‑app Content: Mood check‑ins, notes, personal map/archetype selections, meditation progress.
  • Device Info: App version, basic diagnostics (crash logs) – optional.

How We Use Data

  • Create and manage your account
  • Sync and display your notes and check‑ins
  • Security, performance and bug fixing

Storage & Security

Data is stored on Firebase. Encryption is used in transit and at rest. Only authorized services can access your data.

Sharing

Your data is not sold and is not shared for marketing. If required by law, limited sharing with authorities may occur.

Children’s Privacy

Seyr is not intended for users under 13.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, delete your data
  • Restrict processing and object
  • Data portability

Contact: meliksah@makeryapi.com

Retention

We retain data while your account is active. Upon account deletion, data is permanently deleted within a reasonable period.

International Transfers

Due to Firebase infrastructure, data may be processed in different countries/regions. Appropriate safeguards are applied.

Tracking & Ads

Seyr is ad‑free and does not track users.

Updates

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted here.

Apple “App Privacy” Summary

No Tracking Data Collection: Minimal

  • Data Linked to You: Email (if using Sign in with Apple), in‑app content (notes, check‑ins) – for App Functionality.
  • Data Not Linked to You: Diagnostics (crash data) – for App Improvement (optional).
